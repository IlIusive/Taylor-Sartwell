Capstone

Hydro Hero

· Jan 2023 — Aug 2023 ·

Premis:

For my capstone project at Wentworth Institute of Technology, I designed a robot that can clean water surfaces from pollutants and debris.

  • Developed, assembled, and tested an aquatic robot to combat environmental pollution
  • Collaborated in a team of five mechanical engineering students over 8 months
  • Modeled parts in SolidWorks and created system for collection method
  • 3D printed pieces, rapid prototyped, and tested functions in dry and wet environments
  • Maintained project budget, parts list, and receipts for reimbursement
  • Showcased and demonstrated final working prototype in August 2023

3D Printed Boat

Wind-Up Boat

· May 2023 — Jul 2023 ·

Premis:

Final Project for Additive Manufacturing course. I designed a wind-up mechanism in SolidWorks, integrated it into an assembly, and 3D printed the entire system, including the boat's propeller and top sections. The mechanism is set in motion by a winding key, driving the propeller's rotation when released.
